## 🎯 Problem Statement Alignment
Emergency response systems today suffer from the **"Gap of Panic"**—the critical 10-minute window between a crisis occurring and professional help arriving. During this time, information is unstructured, messy, and multilingual. MediBridge solves this by instantly converting chaotic emergency data into structured, life-saving protocols.
